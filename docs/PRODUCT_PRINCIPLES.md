# Product Principles

> **Japanese summaries for invite/beta readers**: see `docs/INVITE_AT_A_GLANCE.md` → `INVITE_OVERVIEW.md` → `INVITE_DEEP_DIVE.md`. Full doc map: `docs/README.md`.

## Purpose

Nagi SNS aims to provide a calm communication space where people are not evaluated by visible numbers.

## Core Principles

1. Do not evaluate people by numbers.
2. Reduce incentives for hostile behavior by structure.
3. Prioritize psychological safety over growth metrics.
4. Keep product behavior consistent with stated values.

## Non-Goals

- Showing like counts or follower counts.
- Ranking people by account-level score.
- Designing for ad-driven engagement optimization.
- Moderation focused on punishment as a primary goal.

## Decision Criteria

When adding a feature, check:

1. Does this reduce pressure from comparison?
2. Does this lower the spread value of harmful content?
3. Does this improve safety for first-time posters?
4. Is this understandable without hidden rules?

If any answer is "no", reconsider the design before implementation.
